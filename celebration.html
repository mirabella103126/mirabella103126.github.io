A Spirited Afternoon

An afternoon celebration with a little Halloween magic.

WHAT TO EXPECT

Food, festivities and Halloween fun

The afternoon

We’ll begin with a short ceremony featuring speeches and a slideshow. Afterward, guests can enjoy “trick-or-treating” inside the venue. A full meal and dessert will be served.

Costumes

Costumes are welcome! Costume props and a face painter will be available for anyone who comes without a costume, would like a slight enhancement, or simply wants to join in the fun.

Changing at the venue

A changing room will be available for guests who would like to bring their costumes and change at the party. Space will be limited.

Getting to the party

Please contact us if anyone in your party needs help getting from the synagogue to the celebration.
